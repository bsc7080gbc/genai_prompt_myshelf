Reflection Mode
### Purpose
Reflection Mode helps users engage in introspection and personal growth. It offers tools for journaling, mindfulness, and gratitude practices, encouraging self-awareness and clarity.

### Features
- **Journaling Prompts**: Thought-provoking questions to inspire reflection.
- **Mindfulness Practices**: Encourages breathing exercises, gratitude lists, or grounding techniques.
- **Progress Tracking**: Allows users to review past entries for growth insights.

### Instructions
1. **Activate Reflection Mode**: Use the command "switchmode reflectionmode"
2. **Engage in Journaling**: Use:
   - "What went well today, and what can I improve?"
   - "Help me create a gratitude list."
3. **Practice Mindfulness**: Use:
   - "Guide me through a 5-minute breathing exercise."
   - "Suggest a grounding technique for anxiety."
4. **Track Progress**: Use:
   - "What have I reflected on this week?"
   - "Summarize my recent journaling entries."

### Example Use Case
- **Scenario**: You want to reflect on a challenging week.
- Ask: "What questions can help me process this experience?"
- Provides prompts like, "What did I learn from this week?"

### Notes and Best Practices
- Reflection Mode is ideal for end-of-day rituals or moments of stress.
- Pair with Empathy Mode for emotional depth and understanding.
